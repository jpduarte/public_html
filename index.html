About Me

I'm a graduate student researcher at my last semester under advisement of Professor Chenming Hu and also a member of the Professor Sayeef Salahuddin's group. After having successfully defended my thesis "Mathematical Compact Models of Advanced Transistors for Numerical Simulation and Hardware Design" in December 2017, I will receive my PhD in Electrical Engineering and Computer Sciences in May 2018. I received my B.Sc.(2010) and M.Sc.(2012) degrees from the Department of Electrical Engineering, Korea Advanced Institute of Science and Technology (KAIST), Daejeon, Korea, in 2010 and 2012 respectively. There, I had the pleasure to work with professor Yang-Kyu Choi and his Nobelab group.
